tRRD - Row to Row Delay or RAS to RAS Delay

 

"The amount of cycles that it takes to activate the next bank of memory. It is the opposite of tRAS. The lower the
timing, the better the performance, but it can cause instability."


NOTE: tRRDS is the version of this timing when activating a bank in a different bank group, and is the
shorter of the two timings
