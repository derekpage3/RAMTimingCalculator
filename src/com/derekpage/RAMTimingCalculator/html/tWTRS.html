tWTR - Write to Read Delay

 

"The amount of cycles required between a valid write command and the next read command. Lower is better
performance, but can cause instability."


NOTE: tWTRS is the version of this timing when doing a write to one bank group and then a read on a
different bank group, and is the shorter of the two timings.
